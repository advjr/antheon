Individual Exercise Physiology

Personalised support to help you move well, feel stronger, and improve your health. Accredited Exercise Physiologists are university qualified health professionals who specialise in movement for clinical outcomes.

At Optimum Exercise Physiology, our individual consultations are designed around you.

These one-to-one sessions give you dedicated time with an Exercise Physiologist to understand your body, your goals, and the best path forward for your health. Whether you are managing pain, returning to exercise, recovering from injury, living with a chronic condition, or simply wanting expert guidance, we tailor every session to your needs.

This is not a one-size-fits-all gym program. It is safe, clinical, supervised exercise support from highly qualified professionals who use exercise as medicine.

Who are individual consultations for?

Individual exercise physiology consultations may be the right fit for you if:

  • you are unsure where to start with exercise
  • you feel nervous about exercising on your own or in a gym environment
  • you are managing pain, injury, illness, or a chronic health condition
  • you want a personalised program based on your goals and current ability
  • you would like close supervision and guidance with technique
  • you are returning to exercise after time away
  • you need support to build confidence before joining a class or independent gym program
  • you have been referred by your GP or another health professional

What can we help with?

Our Exercise Physiologists work with clients across a wide range of health concerns and goals, including:

  • musculoskeletal pain and injury rehabilitation
  • strength and balance
  • osteoporosis and bone health
  • arthritis and joint pain
  • diabetes and metabolic health
  • Strength and conditioning
  • heart health
  • neurological conditions
  • cancer rehabilitation
  • falls prevention
  • improving mobility, fitness, and function with exercise
  • Athletic performance

Whether your goal is to reduce pain, improve function, build strength, manage a condition, or simply feel better in yourself, we will meet you where you are.

Funding and referral options

We welcome private paying clients, as well as those accessing support through:

  • Medicare referrals
  • NDIS
  • DVA
  • ReturnToWorkSA
  • Sonder
  • Private health insurance
  • Aged Care

If you are not sure whether you are eligible, our team can help guide you.

Ready to get started?

If you are looking for expert support, a tailored plan, and a safe place to exercise, our individual exercise physiology consultations are a great place to begin.
