Personal Bio:

Lisa graduated from the University of South Australia with a Bachelor of Applied Science (Exercise and Sports Science) and then completed a Masters of Clinical Exercise Physiology at Charles Sturt University.

Lisa is the Principal Exercise Physiologist and Director at Optimum Exercise Physiology. She is deeply passionate about the concept of 'Exercise as Medicine' and dedicated to demonstrating how targeted movement can significantly improve a client's health, wellbeing, and overall quality of life.

In addition to her clinical work, Lisa enjoys being active in the local community, advocating for proactive health management through science-based exercise interventions.

Lisa has a keen interest in bone health, having undergone specialized training to become an Onero accredited practitioner, which allows her to provide evidence-based exercise for those at risk of osteoporotic fractures.

When she isn't at the clinic, Lisa enjoys staying active herself and spending time with her family, practicing what she preaches regarding a balanced and healthy lifestyle.
