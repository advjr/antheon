Privacy Policy

私隱政策

Last updated: 23 July 2026
最後更新：2026 年 7 月 23 日

1. Overview

This website (the "Site") is a personal professional profile for Dr. Li Wei. It is informational, and it does not directly process bookings, payments, or patient dental/medical records. It exists to help you learn about Dr. Li Wei's treatments and to make it easy to get in touch.

2. Information We Collect

When you browse the Site, we (or our analytics providers) may automatically collect basic technical information such as your IP address, browser type, referring page, pages viewed, and anonymous analytics events. This is used only to understand how the Site is used and to keep it running well. The Site does not collect personal medical or dental information through ordinary browsing.

3. Booking & Contact Enquiries

If you submit the booking form or message us on WhatsApp, you provide us with information such as your name, phone number, and a short message describing what you need. We use this only to reply to you and arrange your appointment. Once your enquiry is passed to the clinic to be actioned, any further patient record created for your care is handled under the clinic's own patient record obligations under Hong Kong law.

4. Cookies & Analytics

The Site uses cookies for basic, privacy-respecting analytics and measurement. You can choose which categories of cookies you allow using the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer at any time. Non-essential analytics only run if you accept them.

5. Your Rights

Under Hong Kong's Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (PDPO), you have the right to request access to, or correction of, personal data we hold about you in connection with this Site. To make such a request, please contact us using the details below.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. Technical data may be processed by service providers who help us run the Site (such as hosting and analytics providers), under their own confidentiality and data protection obligations.

7. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or how the Site handles your information, please reach out via WhatsApp or the contact details on the Contact section of the main site. For questions about your dental records or ongoing care, please contact the clinic directly.

1. 概覽

本網站（「本網站」）是李蔚醫生的個人專業檔案，屬於資訊性質，並不直接處理預約、付款或病人的牙科／醫療紀錄。本網站旨在讓你了解李蔚醫生提供的治療服務，並方便你與我們聯絡。

2. 我們收集的資料

當你瀏覽本網站時，我們（或我們的分析服務供應商）可能會自動收集基本技術資料，例如你的 IP 位址、瀏覽器類型、來源頁面、瀏覽頁面及匿名分析事件。這些資料僅用於了解網站的使用情況，以確保網站運作良好。本網站在一般瀏覽情況下不會收集個人醫療或牙科資料。

3. 預約及查詢

當你提交預約表格或透過 WhatsApp 聯絡我們時，你會提供例如姓名、電話號碼及簡述需要的資料。我們僅用作回覆你及安排應診。當你的查詢轉交診所跟進後，其後為你的護理而建立的任何病人紀錄，將按香港法律下診所本身的病人紀錄責任處理。

4. Cookies 及分析

本網站使用 cookies 作基本、注重私隱的分析及成效評估。你可隨時透過頁尾的「Cookie 設定」連結，選擇你允許的 cookies 類別。非必要的分析功能，只有在你同意後才會運作。

5. 你的權利

根據香港《個人資料（私隱）條例》，你有權要求查閱或更正我們就本網站所持有關於你的個人資料。如欲提出此類要求，請使用下方的聯絡方式與我們聯絡。

6. 我們如何分享資料

我們不會出售個人資料。技術資料可能會由協助我們營運本網站的服務供應商（例如網站託管及分析服務供應商）處理，並受其本身的保密及資料保護責任所規範。

7. 聯絡我們

如你對本私隱政策或本網站處理你資料的方式有任何疑問，請透過主網站聯絡部分的 WhatsApp 或聯絡方式與我們聯絡。如你對牙科紀錄或持續護理有任何查詢，請直接聯絡診所。
