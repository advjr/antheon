In short

Smart Retirement Blueprint (退「優」方程式) is the personal advisory of Philip Cheng, an independent retirement and legacy advisor in Hong Kong and a Sun Life-certified Wealth & Risk Management Consultant. He helps Hong Kong residents plan retirement finances, structure assets for the Old Age Living Allowance, access Retire3 reverse mortgage referrals, and find trusted Greater Bay Area retirement homes, medical, rehabilitation and dental care, including organised tour visits. Successful Old Age Living Allowance applicants currently receive HK$4,345 per month per person.

退「優」方程式（Smart Retirement Blueprint）是鄭任中的個人顧問品牌。他是香港獨立退休及傳承顧問，亦是永明金融持牌財富及風險管理策劃師，協助香港居民規劃退休財務、為申請長者生活津貼而重整資產、轉介「退休三寶」安老按揭，並尋找可信賴的大灣區安老院舍、醫療、復康及牙科服務，包括安排院舍參觀。成功申請長者生活津貼者，現時每人每月可領取 港幣 4,345 元。

01About Philip關於鄭任中

The person behind the plan.

計劃背後，是一個人。

Philip Cheng (鄭任中) holds a Bachelor's degree in Economics from the University of Western Ontario, Canada, and is a licensed Wealth & Risk Management Consultant in Hong Kong. He serves as Chief Executive of the GBA Elderly Services Development Association and sits on the HKCT Professional Education & Training Committee, working across retirement planning, asset growth, risk management and Greater Bay Area elderly care.

Rooted in Hong Kong, he has watched the genuine hardships of an ageing city: longer lives alongside strained medical and care resources, and most people with little awareness of retirement risk until a crisis arrives. When the Government encouraged retirement in the Greater Bay Area, he visited retirement cities across the region himself, inspecting communities and experiencing the medical and living environment first hand.

In wealth and risk advisory he is an authorised consultant of Sun Life Hong Kong — a company with over 130 years in Hong Kong, USD 1.2 trillion in assets under management and an S&P “AA” rating, one of the few insurers here rated top tier by all four major agencies. It is the partner he trusts most.

From four areas of expertise — Hong Kong asset growth, personal risk management, local welfare policy and premium Greater Bay Area care — he built Smart Retirement Blueprint: one all-in-one, holistic plan rather than fragmented advice. His belief is simple: retirement is not a passive wait, but a stage you can design with clarity and control.

鄭任中先生擁有加拿大西安大略大學經濟學學士學位，是香港持牌財富及風險管理策劃師，現任大灣區老人服務發展協會總幹事，並擔任港專（HKCT）專業教育及培訓委員會委員，深耕退休規劃、資產增值、風險管理及大灣區安老服務。

紮根香港的他，長期觀察到高齡化社會的真實困境：長壽時代來臨、醫療與安老資源緊張，而大多數港人在意外或風險來臨前，往往缺乏退休風險管理意識。響應政府「北上退休」的方向，他親身走訪大灣區退休城市，實地考察院舍，體驗當地的醫療與養老環境。

What is an Enduring Power of Attorney?

甚麼是持久授權書？

An Enduring Power of Attorney lets you appoint someone you trust to manage your finances if you later lose the mental capacity to do so yourself. It keeps decisions in trusted hands rather than leaving them to chance.

持久授權書讓你委任一位信任的人，在你日後喪失處理財務的精神行為能力時，代你管理財務，使重要決定交由可信賴的人，而非聽天由命。

Best for families who want decisions and legacy protected in advance.

適合希望提前守護決策權與傳承的家庭。

What is a reverse mortgage?

甚麼是安老按揭？

A reverse mortgage lets an older homeowner borrow against a property while continuing to live in it, receiving regular payouts instead of making monthly repayments. It can turn a home into retirement income without having to sell or move out.

安老按揭讓年長業主以自住物業作抵押借款，同時繼續居於其中，並收取定期年金，而非每月供款，能在毋須出售或遷出的情況下，把物業轉化為退休收入。

Best for homeowners or policyholders who want retirement income.

適合希望取得退休收入的業主或保單持有人。

What is the Old Age Living Allowance?

甚麼是長者生活津貼？

The Old Age Living Allowance is a Hong Kong Government allowance for eligible older residents, subject to asset and income limits. Successful applicants currently receive HK$4,345 per month per person. Amounts and rules are set by the Government and may change.

長者生活津貼是香港政府為合資格年長居民提供的津貼，須符合資產及入息限額。成功申請者現時每人每月可領取港幣 4,345 元。金額及規則由政府釐定，並可能不時調整。

What is a CCRC?

甚麼是持續照料退休社區（CCRC）？

A Continuing Care Retirement Community (CCRC) brings independent living, assisted care, and nursing care together in one community, so a resident can stay as their needs change over time. It is a growing model across the Greater Bay Area.

持續照料退休社區（CCRC）把自理生活、協助照顧及護理服務集於同一社區，讓住客在需要改變時仍可留在原處。這是大灣區日漸普及的養老模式。

Best for families exploring quality, affordable care beyond Hong Kong.
