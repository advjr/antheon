Who We Service

Introduction to Magna Constructions

A privately owned builder insurers can rely on

Magna Constructions Pty Ltd specialises in building reinstatement repairs after insurable events across the domestic, commercial and industrial sectors. We were formed to bring a genuinely competitive, high-service option to the insurance repair sector.

With over 25 years of industry experience across a wide range of building types, we were formed to provide a competitive service to the insurance repair sector across our coverage areas in South Australia, Victoria, New South Wales, the Australian Capital Territory and Queensland.

Our understanding and high-level focus on service levels, customer satisfaction and repair quality result in positive claim outcomes for our clients and their insureds.

Service 01

24 Hour Emergency Callout

When damage strikes, every hour counts. Our office and emergency line is staffed around the clock, so there is always someone ready to respond and stop a bad situation getting worse.

We mobilise make-safe teams with immediate effect to secure the property and mitigate any further loss before full reinstatement begins.

  • Round the clock make-safe response
  • Emergency board-up and temporary weatherproofing
  • Rapid site stabilisation to prevent secondary damage

Service 02

Building Assessments

Good claims start with an accurate picture of the damage. Our assessments are thorough and honest, giving insurers and insureds the same clear understanding of what needs to happen.

Every assessment produces a detailed scope of works, so pricing is fair, expectations are set, and the reinstatement can move forward without surprises.

  • On-site inspection and damage evaluation
  • Detailed, itemised scope of works
  • Clear reporting for insurer and insured

Service 03

Building Reinstatement

This is the heart of what we do. We reinstate domestic, commercial and industrial buildings damaged by insurable events, from small single-room repairs to large and complex loss.

We use only skilled and qualified staff, manage the project end to end, and keep everyone informed so the property is returned to its pre-loss condition with as little disruption as possible.

  • Domestic, commercial and industrial repairs
  • Small losses through to large and complex claims
  • Full project management and quality control

Service 04

Roofing Services

Storm and impact damage to a roof rarely waits. A compromised roof lets water in and turns one problem into many, so restoring weather-tightness quickly is a priority.

Our roofing teams repair and replace damaged roofing to a high standard, protecting the rest of the building from further loss while the wider claim is completed.

  • Storm and impact roof repair
  • Full roof replacement where required
  • Fast restoration of weather-tightness

How A Claim Works

2
