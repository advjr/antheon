Personal Training

Personalised support to help you move well, feel stronger, and improve your health. Accredited Exercise Physiologists are university qualified health professionals who specialise in movement for clinical, health and fitness outcomes.

Our personal training sessions are designed for people who want to improve their fitness, build strength, tone up, and feel better in their body with the support of an expert by their side. Whether you are getting back into exercise, wanting more structure and accountability, or looking for help to work towards weight loss and better overall health, our team can help you train in a way that is effective, personalised, and sustainable.

At Optimum, your sessions are delivered by university-trained Exercise Physiologists. This means your training is guided by professionals with a deeper understanding of how the body moves, adapts, and responds to exercise. While personal trainers can be a great support in the fitness space, working with an Exercise Physiologist offers an added level of knowledge and individualisation, helping to ensure your program is not only goal-focused, but also appropriate for your body, your history, and your long-term health.

We offer both 1:1 personal training sessions and buddy sessions (1:2), giving you the option to train one-on-one or alongside a partner or friend. Buddy sessions can be a great way to stay motivated, enjoy the social side of exercise, and work towards your goals with support and a lower price point.

Personalised training for real-life goals

No two people are the same, and your training should reflect that. Your program will be tailored to your starting point, fitness level, and personal goals, whether that is improving your general fitness, increasing strength, feeling more toned, boosting energy, or creating healthier habits that support weight management.

We focus on practical, progressive training that fits into real life. That means no gimmicks, no extremes, and no one-size-fits-all approach. Just expert guidance, a clear plan, and support to help you keep moving forward.

Why train with an Exercise Physiologist?

Our Exercise Physiologists bring together fitness expertise and a strong understanding of exercise prescription, movement quality, and progression. For you, that means:

  • a program tailored specifically to your body and goals
  • close attention to technique and movement quality
  • a smarter and more individualised approach to training
  • safe progression as your fitness and confidence improve
  • support from a qualified professional who understands more than just the workout itself

It is personal training with an added layer of expertise.

A supportive environment to help you feel confident

Personal training at Optimum is about more than just exercise. It is about helping you feel comfortable, capable, and supported while working towards your goals. Some people come to us because they want accountability. Others want to learn how to exercise properly, build confidence in the gym, or have someone guide them every step of the way.

Wherever you are starting from, we will meet you there and help you build from it.

Getting started

Your personal training journey begins with an initial appointment so we can understand your goals, exercise background, and what you would like to achieve. From there, we can guide you into one-on-one or buddy personal training sessions designed to help you improve your fitness, strength, and overall health in a way that feels achievable and right for you.

If you would like to feel stronger, fitter, and more confident with expert support, our personal training sessions are a great place to start.
