SEO, AEO, and GEO, in plain English.

How people find a business online has split into three separate games. You used to only need to win one of them. Here is what each one means, and why we build for all three.

SEO gets you found in a list of links. AEO gets your answer pulled into the box at the top of the page. GEO gets your business named by name when someone asks an AI assistant instead of Google. Most businesses are still only playing the first game.

How the shift actually works

These three aren't separate strategies competing for budget, they build on each other.

01

SEO: keep optimising

Product pages, backlinks, site speed. This is still your foundation, everything else builds on top of it.

02

AEO: structure to answer

Write for specific questions. FAQ pages, comparison guides, "best X for Y" articles. Be quotable.

03

GEO: get recommended

Show up where AI actually learns from: reviews, forums, niche publications, expert roundups. Be talked about.

Most brands are still optimising for page one. The ones winning are optimising for the answer.
