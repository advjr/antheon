In short

Dr. Li Wei is a general, cosmetic and implant dentist in Hong Kong with over 12 years of clinical experience. She provides gentle preventive care, cosmetic dentistry such as whitening and porcelain veneers, dental implants and restorations, and clear aligner treatment for teens and adults. She sees nervous patients and children, works at a calm pace, and explains every option and cost before treatment begins.

李蔚醫生是香港的全科、美容及植牙牙醫，擁有超過 12 年臨床經驗，提供溫和的預防護理、美容牙科（如牙齒美白及瓷貼面）、植牙及修復治療，以及為青少年和成人而設的隱形牙套療程。她接見緊張的病人及兒童，步調從容，並在治療前清楚說明每個選擇及費用。

01About Dr. Li Wei關於李蔚醫生

The dentist behind the chair.

牙椅背後，是一個人。

Dr. Li Wei became a dentist because she believes good dental care should never feel rushed or frightening. Over more than a decade in practice, she has cared for families across Hong Kong, from a child's first visit to an adult's full-mouth restoration.

She keeps her list personal, so you see the same dentist each time and the relationship builds over the years. Her training spans general, cosmetic and implant dentistry, and she continues to study each year to keep her techniques current and comfortable.

Most of her patients come by word of mouth, referred by someone who was treated kindly and honestly, and stayed.

李蔚醫生選擇成為牙醫，是因為她相信好的牙科護理，不應令人感到倉促或恐懼。行醫十餘年，她照顧過無數香港家庭，由孩子的第一次應診，到成人的全口修復。

她刻意維持貼身的病人數目，讓你每次都由同一位牙醫診治，讓關係隨歲月建立。她的專業橫跨全科、美容及植牙牙科，並每年持續進修，讓技術與時並進，過程更為舒適。

她的病人大多經口碑轉介而來，由曾被溫柔而坦誠對待的人介紹，並一直留下。

01

General & Preventive Dentistry

全科及預防牙科

Check-ups · cleaning · fillings · root canal
檢查 · 洗牙 · 補牙 · 杜牙根

Routine examinations, professional scaling and polishing, tooth-coloured fillings, and root canal treatment. Dr. Li Wei focuses on catching issues early and keeping your natural teeth healthy for as long as possible.

定期檢查、專業洗牙及拋光、與牙色相近的補牙，以及杜牙根治療。李蔚醫生著重及早發現問題，讓你的天然牙齒盡可能長久保持健康。

Why every six months?

為何每半年檢查一次？

Regular check-ups let a dentist spot decay and gum problems while they are still small, painless, and inexpensive to treat, before they turn into something bigger.

定期檢查讓牙醫在蛀牙及牙周問題仍細小、無痛且治療費用較低時及早發現，避免演變成更大的問題。

Best for the whole family, including children and nervous patients.

適合全家大小，包括兒童及緊張的病人。

02

Cosmetic Dentistry

美容牙科

Whitening · porcelain veneers · bonding
牙齒美白 · 瓷貼面 · 修補

Professional teeth whitening, porcelain veneers, and cosmetic bonding to refresh a smile. Dr. Li Wei favours a natural look, enhancing your own teeth rather than replacing their character.

專業牙齒美白、瓷貼面及美容修補，讓笑容煥然一新。李蔚醫生崇尚自然效果，著重提升你原有牙齒的美感，而非改變其個性。

What are veneers?

甚麼是瓷貼面？

Veneers are thin custom shells bonded to the front of teeth to improve their shape, colour, or alignment. They can correct chips, gaps, and staining with minimal change to the tooth beneath.

瓷貼面是量身訂製的薄殼，黏貼於牙齒前方，以改善其形狀、顏色或排列，能修正崩缺、縫隙及染色，而對底下牙齒的改動極少。

Best for anyone who feels self-conscious about their smile.

適合對自己笑容感到不自在的人。

03

Dental Implants & Restorations

植牙及牙齒修復

Implants · crowns · bridges · dentures
植牙 · 牙冠 · 牙橋 · 假牙

Replacing missing or damaged teeth with dental implants, crowns, bridges, and dentures. Dr. Li Wei restores both function and appearance so you can eat, speak, and smile with confidence.

以植牙、牙冠、牙橋及假牙修復缺失或受損的牙齒。李蔚醫生同時重建功能與外觀，讓你能自信地進食、說話與微笑。

What is a dental implant?

甚麼是植牙？

An implant is a small titanium post placed in the jaw to act as a replacement tooth root, topped with a crown. It looks and functions like a natural tooth and does not rely on neighbouring teeth for support.

植牙是植入顎骨的細小鈦金屬支柱，用作替代牙根，其上再裝上牙冠。它的外觀與功能均如天然牙齒，且毋須依賴鄰牙作支撐。

Best for restoring missing teeth for the long term.

適合長遠修復缺失牙齒。

04

Clear Aligners & Orthodontics

隱形牙套及矯齒

Invisalign · clear aligners · teens & adults
Invisalign · 隱形牙套 · 青少年及成人

Straightening teeth discreetly with clear aligners, suitable for many cases of crowding and spacing. After a scan, Dr. Li Wei shows you the likely result and gives an honest view on whether aligners fit your case.

以隱形牙套低調地矯正牙齒，適用於多種擠迫及縫隙情況。經口腔掃描後，李蔚醫生會向你展示預期效果，並坦誠評估隱形牙套是否適合你的情況。

How do clear aligners work?

隱形牙套如何運作？

Clear aligners are a series of removable, near-invisible trays that gently move teeth into position over time. They can be taken out to eat and clean, making them a discreet alternative to fixed braces.

隱形牙套是一系列可拆卸、近乎隱形的牙托，隨時間溫和地把牙齒移至理想位置。進食及清潔時可取出，是固定牙箍以外低調的選擇。

Best for teens and adults who want straighter teeth discreetly.

適合希望低調地矯正牙齒的青少年及成人。

Honest answers to real questions.

就真實疑問，給予坦誠解答。

Dr. Li Wei offers general and preventive dentistry, cosmetic dentistry, dental implants and restorations, and clear aligner orthodontics.

That covers everything from a routine check-up, cleaning, and fillings to whitening, veneers, implants, crowns, and teeth straightening for teens and adults, all under one trusted dentist.

李蔚醫生提供全科及預防牙科、美容牙科、植牙及修復治療，以及隱形牙套矯齒服務。

對大部分人而言，每半年檢查及洗牙一次，能保持牙齒及牙齦健康，並及早發現問題。

部分複雜個案以固定牙箍治療更佳，或需轉介專科。若屬此情況，李蔚醫生會坦誠相告，而非勉強開展難有理想效果的療程。
