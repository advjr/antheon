What is Exercise Physiology?

Accredited Exercise Physiologists (AEPs) are university qualified allied health professionals. We specialise in exercise prescription and the delivery of evidence-based movement programs for the treatment and prevention of chronic diseases.

Book Consultation Learn More

We are the Experts in Exercise

Accredited Exercise Physiologists help people to manage and improve a broad range of conditions including diabetes, heart disease, obesity, chronic respiratory diseases, osteoporosis, arthritis, cancer, and musculoskeletal injuries.

No other allied health professional undertakes the university level training to the depth of Exercise Physiologists in these chronic conditions, making us the primary experts in clinical movement.

Whether you're recovering from surgery or managing a lifelong condition, we provide the roadmap to help you move with confidence again.
